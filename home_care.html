Home Care

What are Home Care Products used for?

Our skin is exposed to a lot of dust, dirt and pollution throughout the day. This impacts our skin texture and can even cause skin problems. Daily skin care routine helps in getting rid of oil and dirt thereby opening the clogged pores that lead to pimples, blackheads and other skin problems. This simple daily routine is also recommended to enhance the look.

Types of Home Care Products

  • Disinfectant Spray: With the deadly virus around and germs that we have to face, we feel the need for disinfectant sprays more than ever. There are a variety of sprays available in the market and you can use them to keep your home germs and virus free. These are usually multipurpose and very effective.
  • Antiseptic Liquid: Not only for first aid but antiseptic liquids can also be used for surface cleaning, personal hygiene as well as hand sanitizer. This is a must-buy product for you and your family and can even be mixed with bathing water for all-around protection from illness-causing germs
  • Floor Cleaner Liquid: These are usually disinfectant liquids available in various fragrances for a nice smelling home, it can be used directly or diluted in water, perfect for cleaning floors, tiles, sinks, kitchen counters and all other washable surfaces. It removes microorganisms and helps you practice good hygiene and cleanliness..

Top Home Care Product Brands & USPs

No single product can provide optimum performance. Thus, different house care products are available in the market and are formulated to clean efficiently and conveniently. Some are designed for general cleaning, while others work best on specific surfaces and hard stains. Brands like Dettol, Harpic, Lizol, Comfort, Savlon are widely preferred.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the common ingredients present in-home care products?

Ingredients such as abrasives, acids, alkalies, bleaches, sanitizers, spirit solvents and other chemicals are commonly present in-home care products.

Does disinfectant spray have alcohol?

Yes, disinfectant sprays have a broad spectrum of antimicrobial activity with a fair percentage of ethyl alcohol for effective germ protection.

Does mosquito repellent expire?

Yes, home care products are usually safe. However, you must always be aware and careful while using them, read the labels, follow instructions and always wash your hands after use.
